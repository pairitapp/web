What is PairIt ?

Who would've thought learning languages can be fun?

You must try to match each word with its translation as fast as possible!

Team

In a team of two, Rodolphe and Adam, we built the entire app with SwiftUI. After months of work, we finally released PairIt on the App Store on October 27, 2022.

We had the idea to build this app while trying to learn a new language. We were looking for an app that would help us learn vocabulary rapidly and efficiently. We couldn't find any, so we built one!

For the sound design and art, all the credits go to Elvis a.k.a. CherryLondon, Remi and April.

How it works

In each game, there are three levels which are in increasing difficulty. A level is completed once the user has seen all of the words in that level. To show a user new words, we use rounds.

Each round contains six words and their translation. The algorithm was built in a way such that you can play forever!

First, we built a database of the words and their translation. To do so, we collected a list of the most frequent 2000 words in English and then translated them into 100+ languages.

To decide which words to show the user, we built a spaced repetition algorithm based on the time taken to match two words together. This means that in any round, some words will be new while others have already been seen.
